Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can we start services?

We typically schedule initial assessments within 1-2 weeks of your inquiry. Services can begin shortly after the assessment is completed and treatment plans are developed.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we work with most major insurance providers. Our team will help verify your benefits and handle the insurance process for you.

What should I expect in the first session?

The first session is a comprehensive assessment where we'll observe your child, discuss goals with you, and begin developing an individualized treatment plan.

Can parents observe sessions?

Absolutely! We encourage caregiver involvement and provide training so you can support your child's progress at home.
