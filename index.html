Our Services

We offer comprehensive ABA therapy services designed to meet the unique needs of each child and family we serve.

Early Intervention

Specialized programs for children ages 2-6 focusing on communication, social skills, and behavior management.

  • Individualized treatment plans
  • Parent training included
  • Play-based learning

School Readiness

Programs designed to prepare children for successful integration into school environments.

  • Classroom behavior skills
  • Group instruction readiness
  • Academic foundational skills

In-Home Therapy

Personalized therapy sessions conducted in the comfort of your home for optimal learning.

  • Convenient scheduling
  • Natural environment teaching
  • Family involvement

About Learn by Design

Founded in 2015, Learn by Design has been providing high-quality Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) therapy to children with autism spectrum disorder and related developmental differences.

Our team of Board Certified Behavior Analysts (BCBAs) and Registered Behavior Technicians (RBTs) are dedicated to creating meaningful, lasting change through individualized treatment plans.

Our Mission

To empower children with autism to reach their full potential through scientifically validated ABA therapy, while supporting families with compassion and expertise.
