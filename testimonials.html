RL

Rebecca L.

Parent of 5-year-old Emma

"When Emma was first diagnosed with autism at age 3, we didn't know where to turn. She wasn't speaking, had frequent meltdowns, and struggled with daily routines. Learn by Design became our lifeline.
The team created a comprehensive plan that addressed Emma's communication needs, behavioral challenges, and social development. They didn't just work with Emma - they taught us how to be effective partners in her progress.
Today, Emma is thriving in kindergarten. She communicates her needs clearly, has made friends, and approaches new situations with confidence. The transformation has been nothing short of miraculous.
We're not just grateful for the skills Emma has learned - we're grateful for the hope and confidence Learn by Design gave our entire family."
