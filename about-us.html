About Learn by Design

Dedicated to empowering children with autism through evidence-based ABA therapy and compassionate family support.

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Learn by Design has been providing high-quality Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) therapy to children with autism spectrum disorder and related developmental differences.

Our team of Board Certified Behavior Analysts (BCBAs) and Registered Behavior Technicians (RBTs) are dedicated to creating meaningful, lasting change through individualized treatment plans.

We believe that every child has the potential to learn and grow, and we're committed to helping them reach their full potential through evidence-based practices and compassionate care.

Our Mission

To empower children with autism to reach their full potential through scientifically validated ABA therapy, while supporting families with compassion and expertise.

Compassionate Care

We approach every child and family with empathy, understanding, and respect.

Data-Driven Results

Our methods are grounded in scientific research and proven outcomes.

Family-Centered

We involve families in every step of the therapy process.
