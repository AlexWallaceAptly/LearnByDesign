About Learn by Design

Dedicated to empowering children with autism through evidence-based ABA therapy and compassionate family support.

Our Story

Bailey Bakken, M.S., BCBA and Kayla Wallace, M.S., BCBA co-founded Learn by Design with the mission to provide quality services utilizing progressive ABA ideologies to increase access to compassionate, individualized care and to make meaningful improvements in the lives of families. Together, they take the lead in inspiring others to embrace these principles.

Our team of Board Certified Behavior Analysts (BCBAs) and Registered Behavior Technicians (RBTs) are dedicated to creating meaningful, lasting change through individualized treatment plans.

We believe that every child has the potential to learn and thrive. We're committed to helping each individual reach their full potential through evidence-based practices and compassionate quality care.

Our Mission

To empower individuals with developmental differences to reach their full potential through evidence based ABA practices, while supporting families with compassion and expertise.

Compassionate Care

We approach every child and family with empathy, understanding, and respect.

Data-Driven Results

Our methods are grounded in scientific research and proven outcomes.

Family-Centered

We involve families in every step of the therapy process.
